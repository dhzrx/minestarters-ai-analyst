# Regulatory Frameworks for Mining Token Offerings

## United States

### Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC)
- **Howey Test**: Most mining revenue tokens likely classified as securities
- **Exemptions available**:
  - **Reg D 506(b)**: Up to unlimited raise, accredited investors, no general solicitation
  - **Reg D 506(c)**: Unlimited raise, accredited only, general solicitation OK, must verify
  - **Reg S**: Offshore offering to non-US persons
  - **Reg A+**: Up to $75M, Tier 2, non-accredited OK, SEC qualification required
- **Form D filing**: Required within 15 days of first sale
- **Blue Sky**: State notice filings required (most states)

### CFTC Considerations
- Commodity-linked tokens may trigger CFTC jurisdiction
- Physical delivery exemption may apply for backed tokens

### FinCEN
- MSB registration may be required for token issuers/exchanges
- BSA/AML compliance program required

## European Union

### MiCA (Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation)
- **Effective**: December 2024 (full application)
- **ART Classification**: Asset-Referenced Tokens (backed by commodities)
  - Requires authorization from EU member state
  - Reserve requirements: 1:1 backing, segregated custody
  - White paper approval required
  - Redemption right at any time at market value
  - Reserve composition rules (30% cash at credit institutions)
- **Significant ART**: Additional requirements if AUM > €5B or >10M holders

### MiFID II
- Security tokens fall under MiFID II as transferable securities
- Requires prospectus or exemption
- Authorized intermediaries required for distribution

## United Kingdom

### FCA
- Security tokens: Subject to Prospectus Regulation
- Exemptions: Qualified investors, <150 persons per EEA state, <€8M total
- Crypto asset promotions: FCA approval required since Oct 2023
- High-risk investment classification likely

## Australia

### ASIC
- Mining tokens likely "financial products" under Corporations Act
- Requires AFSL (Australian Financial Services License) or exemption
- Sophisticated investor exemption available (>A$2.5M net assets)
- Product Disclosure Statement (PDS) required for retail

## Singapore

### MAS
- Payment Services Act and Securities and Futures Act apply
- Capital Markets Services license for dealing in securities
- Accredited investor exemption available (>S$2M net assets)
- Technology sandbox available for innovative structures

## Key Compliance Requirements Summary

| Requirement | US | EU | UK | AU | SG |
|-------------|----|----|----|----|-----|
| Securities registration/exemption | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| KYC/AML | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Accredited investors only | Often | No (MiCA) | Often | Often | Often |
| Reserve requirements | No (SEC) | Yes (MiCA) | No | No | No |
| White paper/prospectus | PPM | Yes | Yes | PDS | Yes |
| Ongoing reporting | Annual | Quarterly | Semi-annual | Annual | Semi-annual |
