### Staking Parameters
- Minimum lock: 30 days
- Maximum lock: 365 days (2x yield multiplier)
- Unstaking cooldown: 7 days
- Reward distribution: Daily, auto-compound option
- Slashing: None (not a validator network)

## Buyback & Burn

- 20% of platform revenue used to buy MINE on open market
- Purchased MINE sent to burn address (0x000...dead)
- Burns executed weekly via automated smart contract
- Burn transparency: All transactions verifiable on-chain
- Projected annual burn: ~1-3% of total supply at mature AUM levels

## Governance

- 1 MINE = 1 vote (staked MINE only)
- Proposal threshold: 100,000 MINE
- Quorum: 10% of staked supply
- Voting period: 7 days
- Timelock: 48 hours after vote passes
- Governable parameters: Fee rates, basket composition, treasury allocation, new asset onboarding
