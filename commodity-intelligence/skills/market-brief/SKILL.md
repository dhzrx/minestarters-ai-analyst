# Mining Market Brief

## Purpose

Compile a concise, actionable market intelligence brief covering the key developments in mining and commodities. Designed for investors, project managers, and analysts who need to stay informed on market conditions.

## Brief Structure

### 1. Commodity Price Summary
- Major metals prices (Au, Ag, Cu, Ni, Zn, Li, Fe)
- Notable price movements (>2% daily or >5% weekly)
- Key drivers behind moves
- Forward curve direction (contango/backwardation)

### 2. Major Transactions & Deals
- M&A activity (acquisitions, mergers, JVs)
- Streaming and royalty deals
- Offtake agreements
- Strategic investments
- Deal size and premium/discount analysis

### 3. Project Milestones
- New resource estimates or upgrades
- Feasibility study releases
- Construction starts or completions
- First production announcements
- Mine closures or suspensions

### 4. Corporate News
- Earnings highlights from major miners
- Management changes at key companies
- Dividend announcements
- Guidance revisions (production, cost)

### 5. Policy & Regulation
- Government policy changes affecting mining
- Environmental regulation updates
- Trade policy (tariffs, export controls)
- Permitting decisions (approvals/rejections)

### 6. Sector Outlook
- Near-term sentiment (bullish/neutral/bearish)
- Key events on the horizon (economic data, earnings, conferences)
- Technical levels to watch for major commodities
- Seasonal patterns

## Data Sources

- **Commodity prices**: LME, COMEX, LBMA, Fastmarkets, Asian Metal
- **News**: Mining.com, Reuters, Bloomberg, company announcements
- **Filings**: SEC EDGAR, ASX, TSX/SEDAR
- **Analysis**: Bank research notes, industry reports
- **Use web search** to gather latest information

## Workflow

### Step 1: Price Scan
- Pull latest commodity prices via web search
- Identify notable movers
- Check for supply/demand news driving prices

### Step 2: News Scan
- Search for major mining transactions and deals
- Search for project milestone announcements
- Search for corporate news from major miners
- Search for policy/regulatory developments

### Step 3: Analysis
- Contextualize news within broader market trends
- Identify implications for Minestarters basket holdings
- Highlight actionable intelligence

### Step 4: Compile Brief
- Structure per template below
- Keep each item to 2-3 sentences
- Lead with most impactful items
- Include source references
