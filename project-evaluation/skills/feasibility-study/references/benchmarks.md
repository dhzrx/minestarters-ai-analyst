## Rules of Thumb

- Processing plant capex scales at approximately 0.6 power factor (60% of cost for 50% of capacity)
- Underground mining typically 2-3x more expensive per tonne than open pit
- HPAL nickel plants are among the most capital-intensive in mining
- Contingency should match study stage accuracy (±15% = 15% contingency)
- Owner's costs typically 8-12% of direct capex
- EPCM fees typically 10-15% of direct capex
