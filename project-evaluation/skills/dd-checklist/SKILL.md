# Mining Due Diligence Checklist

## Purpose

Generate a structured due diligence checklist for evaluating mining projects, or score an existing project against the checklist. Covers all aspects a sophisticated investor or acquirer would evaluate.

## Checklist Categories

### 1. Technical
- [ ] Resource/reserve estimate by independent QP/CP
- [ ] Resource classification breakdown (M/I/I)
- [ ] Metallurgical testwork (bench, pilot, demo plant)
- [ ] Mining method validation
- [ ] Geotechnical assessment
- [ ] Hydrogeological study
- [ ] Mine plan and production schedule
- [ ] Processing flowsheet and plant design
- [ ] Tailings and waste management plan
- [ ] Mine closure plan

### 2. Legal & Regulatory
- [ ] Mining license/tenement validity and expiry
- [ ] Title chain and ownership verification
- [ ] Royalty obligations (government and third-party)
- [ ] Environmental permits and approvals
- [ ] Water rights
- [ ] Land access agreements
- [ ] Native title / indigenous rights compliance
- [ ] Pending litigation or disputes
- [ ] Joint venture or partnership agreements

### 3. Financial
- [ ] Audited financial statements (3 years)
- [ ] Feasibility study economics (NPV, IRR, payback)
- [ ] Capital cost estimate (accuracy level stated)
- [ ] Operating cost estimate and benchmarking
- [ ] Revenue model and commodity price assumptions
- [ ] Debt facilities and covenants
- [ ] Hedging positions
- [ ] Tax structure and jurisdiction
- [ ] Insurance coverage

### 4. Environmental, Social & Governance (ESG)
- [ ] Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) approved
- [ ] Baseline environmental studies completed
- [ ] Community engagement and social license
- [ ] Resettlement Action Plan (if required)
- [ ] Community Development Agreement
- [ ] Artisanal mining conflicts assessed
- [ ] GHG emissions and climate risk assessment
- [ ] Water stewardship plan
- [ ] Board composition and governance standards
- [ ] Anti-corruption compliance (FCPA, UK Bribery Act)

### 5. Operations (for producing assets)
- [ ] Production history and reconciliation
- [ ] Equipment condition and remaining life
- [ ] Workforce skills and labor relations
- [ ] Key person dependencies
- [ ] Supply chain reliability
- [ ] Health and safety record (LTIFR, TRIFR)

## Scoring

Each item scored:
- **3 — Green**: Fully satisfied, documented, low risk
- **2 — Yellow**: Partially satisfied, some gaps, moderate risk
- **1 — Red**: Not satisfied, significant gaps, high risk
- **0 — N/A**: Not applicable to this project stage

## Output Format

```

```
