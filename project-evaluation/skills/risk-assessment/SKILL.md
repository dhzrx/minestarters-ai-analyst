# Mining Project Risk Assessment

## Purpose

Identify, evaluate, and prioritize risks for mining projects across all dimensions. Provide likelihood/impact scoring and recommend mitigation strategies.

## Risk Categories

### 1. Technical Risk
- **Geological**: Resource model uncertainty, grade variability, structural complexity
- **Mining**: Geotechnical failure, water ingress, ground conditions
- **Metallurgical**: Recovery shortfall, process variability, deleterious elements
- **Infrastructure**: Power supply, water availability, transport logistics

### 2. Environmental Risk
- **Permitting**: Delays, conditions, rejection
- **Water management**: Acid mine drainage, water scarcity, contamination
- **Tailings**: Dam stability, capacity, closure liability
- **Biodiversity**: Protected species, habitat offset requirements
- **Climate**: Physical risks (floods, drought), transition risks (carbon pricing)

### 3. Social & Political Risk
- **Community**: Opposition, social license, benefit sharing disputes
- **Government**: Policy changes, nationalization, resource nationalism
- **Corruption**: FCPA/UK Bribery Act compliance
- **Security**: Conflict zones, artisanal mining conflicts
- **Labor**: Strikes, skills shortage, local content requirements

### 4. Market Risk
- **Commodity price**: Volatility, structural decline, substitution
- **Currency**: Exchange rate exposure (especially for non-USD cost base)
- **Input costs**: Energy, reagents, explosives, labor inflation
- **Offtake**: Counterparty risk, contract terms

### 5. Financial Risk
- **Funding**: Equity dilution, debt availability, cost overruns
- **Construction**: Budget overrun, schedule delay
- **Cash flow**: Working capital, stockpile management
- **Fiscal**: Tax changes, royalty increases, windfall taxes

## Scoring Matrix

| Score | Likelihood | Impact |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| 1 | Rare (<5%) | Negligible |
| 2 | Unlikely (5-20%) | Minor |
| 3 | Possible (20-50%) | Moderate |
| 4 | Likely (50-80%) | Major |
| 5 | Almost Certain (>80%) | Catastrophic |

**Risk Score** = Likelihood x Impact (1-25 scale)
- 1-4: Low (accept)
- 5-9: Medium (monitor)
- 10-15: High (mitigate)
- 16-25: Critical (avoid or transfer)
