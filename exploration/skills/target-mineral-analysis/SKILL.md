# Target Mineral Analysis

## Purpose

Provide comprehensive analysis of a specific mineral commodity to inform exploration targeting, project evaluation, and investment decisions.

## Supported Minerals

- **Battery metals**: Lithium (Li), Cobalt (Co), Nickel (Ni), Manganese (Mn), Graphite
- **Base metals**: Copper (Cu), Zinc (Zn), Lead (Pb), Tin (Sn)
- **Precious metals**: Gold (Au), Silver (Ag), Platinum (Pt), Palladium (Pd)
- **Critical minerals**: Rare Earth Elements (REE), Tungsten (W), Vanadium (V), Uranium (U)
- **Bulk commodities**: Iron Ore (Fe), Bauxite/Alumina, Manganese Ore

## Workflow

### Step 1: Geology of Deposits
- Major deposit types for this mineral
- Key geological controls and formation conditions
- Grade and tonnage ranges by deposit type
- Notable global deposits and their characteristics

### Step 2: Extraction & Processing
- Mining methods (open pit vs. underground, ISR, brine extraction)
- Processing/beneficiation methods
- Recovery rates by ore type
- Environmental considerations specific to this mineral

### Step 3: Supply Analysis
- Global production by country and company
- Mine pipeline (projects in development)
- Recycling and secondary supply
- Supply chain bottlenecks and concentration risks

### Step 4: Demand Analysis
- End-use sectors and demand breakdown
- Growth drivers (EV transition, energy storage, defense, etc.)
- Substitution risks
- Demand projections (5-year, 10-year)

### Step 5: Price & Market Outlook
- Historical price trends and current price
- Price forecasts from major analysts
- Key price drivers and sensitivities
- Contract structures (spot vs. long-term)
