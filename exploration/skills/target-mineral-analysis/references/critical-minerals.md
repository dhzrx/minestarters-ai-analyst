# Critical Minerals Reference

## US Critical Minerals List (USGS 2022)

50 minerals deemed critical to US economic and national security:

### Energy Transition Minerals
- **Lithium**: Batteries, ceramics — Major producers: Australia, Chile, China
- **Cobalt**: Battery cathodes — Major producers: DRC, Indonesia, Australia
- **Nickel**: Battery cathodes, stainless steel — Major producers: Indonesia, Philippines, Russia
- **Graphite**: Battery anodes — Major producers: China, Mozambique, Brazil
- **Manganese**: Battery cathodes, steel — Major producers: South Africa, Gabon, Australia
- **Rare Earth Elements**: Magnets, electronics — Major producers: China, Myanmar, Australia

### Technology & Defense Minerals
- **Gallium**: Semiconductors — Major producer: China (98%)
- **Germanium**: Fiber optics, IR optics — Major producer: China (60%)
- **Tungsten**: Tooling, defense — Major producer: China (84%)
- **Vanadium**: Steel alloys, flow batteries — Major producers: China, Russia, South Africa

## EU Critical Raw Materials Act (2024)

Benchmarks by 2030:
- 10% EU extraction of annual consumption
- 40% EU processing
- 25% EU recycling
- No more than 65% from any single third country

## Policy Drivers

- **US Inflation Reduction Act (IRA)**: Tax credits tied to domestic/FTA mineral sourcing
- **EU CRMA**: Strategic projects fast-tracking, stockpiling requirements
- **Australia Critical Minerals Strategy**: A$2B+ in financing facilities
- **Canada Critical Minerals Strategy**: C$3.8B investment
